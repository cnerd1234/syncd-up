Osu

Where It Began

Release in 2007 it has been prominent in the PC rhythm gaming scene for its intense gameplay. Users are able to add their own beatmaps and compete against eachother freely, making it a fun competitve soundscape for players looking for a thrill.

How to Play

There is multiple different game modes players can choose from on Osu. The main one being the Osu! the standard game mode most maps use. Using either a mouse or tablet pen players must hit the circle when the outer cirlce aligns on the beat of the music by hitting right click or their keyboard.

Accessible Can be played on multiple platforms such as PCs and tablets (preferred)
Media The game is very community driven so there is a constant stream of new maps and songs being added every day
Upgrades Maintenance happens frequently because of the active community, so theres less worries about bugs.
