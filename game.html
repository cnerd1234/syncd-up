Where It Began

First being released by Konami in Japan in 1998, the series is easily one of the most recognizable rhthym games there is following the global release a year later. Being acclaimed for incorporating fitness into the gameplay it went on to inspiring the use of this thriving gaming model for many other games in the future.

How to Play

The gameplay consists of players using the dancepad to step in time with the music. The dancepad contains arrows that go in four different directions:left, down, up, and right. Watching the screen on either the machine cabinet or their own, players then have to step on the arrow that aligns with the target arrows at the top of the sceen.

Accessible Quality dancepads are difficult to obtain if you wish to play the game the way its intended.
Media Depending on which DDR version your playing, the graphics and music can be dated.
Upgrades DDR is no longer recieving new releases. Community members often mod the games for players to enjoy renewed gameplay.
